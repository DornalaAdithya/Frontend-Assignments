Hyderabad

Hyderabad is the capital city of Telangana and is popularly known as the "City of Pearls". It is famous for its rich history, beautiful monuments like Charminar and Golconda Fort, and delicious Hyderabadi Biryani. The city is also a major IT hub of India, often called "Cybearabad" because many technology companies and startups are located here. Hyderabad blends traditional culture with modern development.

Hyderabad is one of the fastest growing metropolitan cities in India. It is well known for its friendly culture, historic heritage, and modern infrastructure. Popular places like Hussain Sagar Lake, Ramoji Film City, and Salar Jung Museum attract many tourists every year. The city is also an important center for education and technology, hosting many universities and global IT companies. Hyderabad offers a unique mix of tradition and innovation.
